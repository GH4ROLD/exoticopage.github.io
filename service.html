Tips for submitting your music

When it comes to producing a summery sound with Bossa Nova, Jazz and Tropical influences, focus on incorporating instruments such as acoustic guitar, piano, and percussion, such as congas and bongos.

In addition to uploading your music to streaming platforms, it is important to have a presence on social media platforms like Instagram and TikTok, where you can share sneak peeks, behind-the-scenes content, and other materials that give your audience a more personal view of the artist.

To make your music stand out, it is important to focus on the quality of sound and production. If you don't have experience in this area, consider working with a music producer who can help you give your songs a professional touch.
